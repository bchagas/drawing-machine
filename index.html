The Drawing Machine

Author: Bruno Chagas

The Drawing Machine is an APP that helps you to draw a parallelogram from 3 selected points.

Usage

Click on browser area to create a point, after you have created 3 points, the fourth point will be automatically calculated in order to draw a blue parallelogram with the corners in the center of the points that you have chosen, in addition a yellow circle with the same area and center of mass of the parallelogram.

If you align the points to make a square the program will re-create the parallelogram :D.

PA: Parallelogram Area

CA: Cirlce Area

CP: Circle Position
